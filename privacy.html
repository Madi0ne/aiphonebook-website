Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Intellispace Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AIPhoneBook application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Contact Data

AIPhoneBook accesses your device contacts with your explicit permission. This data is processed locally on your device for the purpose of organizing, analyzing, and enriching your contact information.

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect your name and email address to provide account management and support services.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymized usage statistics to improve the app experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our contact management services
  • To detect and merge duplicate contacts
  • To provide AI-powered insights and recommendations
  • To send event reminders for birthdays and important dates
  • To provide secure cloud backup (with your consent)
  • To improve our services through anonymized analytics

Data Storage & Security

Your contact data is primarily stored on your device. If you opt into cloud backup, your data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption both at rest and in transit. We employ appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data.

AI Processing

All AI analysis of your contacts is performed on your device. No personal contact data is transmitted to external AI services. AI features are designed to enhance your experience while maintaining complete data privacy.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your contact data with advertisers or data brokers.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Opt out of analytics and non-essential data collection
  • Revoke contact access permissions at any time

Data Deletion

You may request the deletion of your data by contacting us at support@aiphonebook.app. Upon receiving a verified request, we will delete your data within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
