Frequently Asked Questions

Will moved contacts lose their phone numbers or details?

No. All contact information â€” phone numbers, emails, notes, photos â€” is preserved exactly as it was. The contact simply moves from iOS Contacts to AIPhoneBook's database.

What if the AI gets it wrong?

The AI provides a recommendation, but you make the final decision. Every contact shows the AI's reasoning and confidence level so you can make an informed choice. If you disagree, simply swipe the opposite direction.

Does this affect my ability to receive calls?

No. Moving a contact to AIPhoneBook does not block calls. The contact's number still works normally, and CallerID will still identify them when they call.

Can I run Privacy Shield again later?

Yes. As you add new contacts to your phone, you can run Privacy Shield again to review the new additions. Previously reviewed contacts are not shown again unless you reset your review history.

Does this work with WhatsApp Business contacts?

Privacy Shield reviews contacts stored in iOS Contacts. WhatsApp Business contacts that exist only within WhatsApp are not affected.

What about contacts shared across iCloud?

If you use iCloud Contacts, moving a contact to AIPhoneBook removes it from iCloud sync as well. The contact will no longer appear on your other Apple devices' Contacts app, but CallerID on your iPhone will still work.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. All processing happens on your device. No contact data is transmitted to AIPhoneBook's servers, third-party services, or anywhere else.

How accurate is the AI?

Privacy Shield learns from your decisions. The more contacts you review, the better it understands your preferences. Initial accuracy typically ranges from 80-90% for contacts with clear service signals (role keywords in name, minimal data profile).
