Data Deletion

Last updated: February 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the complete deletion of your AIPhoneBook account and all associated data at any time.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email)
  • Cloud backup data (if you opted in)
  • Usage analytics associated with your account
  • Any preferences or settings stored on our servers

What Stays on Your Device

Your original contacts remain on your device as they are managed by your operating system's contacts framework. AIPhoneBook does not modify or delete your native contacts during account deletion.

How to Request

To request data deletion, please email us at support@aiphonebook.app with:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your full name for verification purposes

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving a verified request. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently deleted.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at support@aiphonebook.app.
